1Introduction

Welcome to the Seraj Al-Din Islamic App (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand our privacy practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process information in connection with the App.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to our data practices.

2Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: When you use the "Contact Us" feature, we collect your message and any contact information you voluntarily provide to communicate with you.
  • Search Queries: Your search queries within the App to improve content discovery and provide relevant results.
  • Content Interactions: Information about which articles, videos, sounds, and books you interact with, including views, downloads, and engagement metrics.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and device settings.
  • Usage Data:
    • Pages visited and content accessed
    • Time spent on different sections
    • Download history
    • Playback duration and positions
    • Click-through data
    • Crash reports and error logs
  • Network Information:
    • IP address (for content delivery optimization)
    • Internet service provider information
    • Browser type and version
  • Local Storage Data:
    • Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
    • Fullscreen mode settings
    • Cached content and images
    • Download history stored locally on your device

2.3 Permissions Required

Permission Purpose Platform

4Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the App's services and comply with applicable laws:

  • Contact Information: Retained for 30 days unless you request deletion
  • Usage Data & Analytics: Retained for up to 12 months for analysis purposes
  • Downloaded Content: Stored locally on your device until you delete them
  • Preferences & Settings: Retained until you uninstall the App or clear the App's cache
  • Crash Reports & Logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
Note: You can clear the App's data, cache, and settings at any time through your device's application settings.

6Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between the App and our API servers (https://api.srajalden.com/api) is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols
  • Secure Communication: All API requests use secure HTTPS connections exclusively
  • Input Validation: Strict validation and sanitization of user input
  • Error Handling: Secure error messages that don't expose sensitive information
  • Logging Security: Sensitive data is never logged in production environments
  • Regular Updates: Continuous monitoring and updates of dependencies for security patches

6.2 Data Storage

  • Local Storage: User preferences are stored securely on your device using SharedPreferences
  • Server Storage: Content and usage data are stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Downloaded Content: Media files are stored in your device's secure storage directories

6.3 Limitations

⚠️ Security Note: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

8Children's Privacy

The App is designed for Islamic knowledge seekers of all ages, including children and minors. However:

  • No Direct Collection from Children: We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Parental Responsibility: Parents and guardians should supervise their children's use of the App
  • No Account Required: The App does not require user registration or account creation
  • Age Restrictions: Some content may be age-appropriate and parents should review content with their children
If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without consent, please contact us immediately.

12Additional Information by Region

12.1 GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent and our legitimate interests in providing the App
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You can lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

12.2 CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal information we collect
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights
